Season-By-Season Comparer

With the season-by-season comparer, you can pick any two players in NBA history (1950-2021) and view all their season statistics for their careers. You can compare any two seasons for any two players and view normal statistics or advanced statistics, with checkmarks next to the player with the better statistic.

Adjusted Statistics Comparer

To better compare players from different time periods, the adjusted statistics comparer standardizes each player's season statistics based on the year's averages and then rescales based on 2020 statistics, so now you can compare any two players as if they played together.

Historical Statistics

In the historical statistics section, you can view the statistics of every player in any given season. You can pick any season, and sort the table by any statistic to see league leaders in any category.

Statistic Predictor (Coming Soon)

The statistic predictor takes advantage of machine learning algorithms in order to estimate 2022 season statistics for all 2021 NBA players.
